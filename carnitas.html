Carnitas

Slow cooker pork carnitas. Serves 4.

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Pork Butt 2 pounds
Salt 1 tbsp
Oregano 1 tbsp
Cumin 2 tsp
Pepper 2 tsp
Bay Leaves 2
Jalapenos 2
White Onion 1/2
Garlic 1 piece
Lime 1
Orange Juice 1/2 cup

Instructions

  1. Cut the pork into half pound pieces. If there is a bone, leave it in.
  2. Cover the pieces in salt, oregano, cumin, and pepper. Place in cooker.
  3. Cut the jalapenos and onion half into large pieces.
  4. Peel the garlic and crush each clove.
  5. Add the jalapenos, onion, and garlic.
  6. Add the OJ and squeeze one lime in.
  7. Put the bay leaves on top.
  8. Close the lid and cook on low for 10 hours.
  9. â€¦
  10. Remove and discard the bay leaves. Same for the bone if there was one.
  11. Move the pork, peppers, onion, and garlic to a sauce pan.
  12. Add some of the pork fat to the pan.
  13. Cook on the stovetop at medium/high heat
  14. Break apart the pork and veggies as they cook.
  15. Cook until a little after the fat cooks off and pork starts to brown.
  16. Finish cooking after 5-10 minutes.
